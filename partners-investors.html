Investors

Elevar Equity is a thesis based investor focused on generating outstanding investment returns by delivering essential services to disconnected communities underserved by global networks. Our entrepreneurs provide massively scalable essential services, tailored explicitly to the needs of their customers to build lasting companies and foster economic development. Our thematic investing has catalysed billions of dollars of capital for millions of customers in order to democratize essential services. Elevar Website

Saama Capital II, is an India focused venture capital fund and is the independent successor fund to SVB India Capital Partners, I ("SICP"). SICP has been successfully investing in Indian companies since 2006 following a collaborative partner driven approach with diversification across stage and sector. Saama Capital invests in exceptional entrepreneurs and ideas to build great companies, and provides support and strength to entrepreneurs to create billion dollar companies in India. For over six years, the Saama team has been closely involved with many successful Indian companies. This list includes names such as Tutorvista, Naaptol, One97 Communications, iYogi, Shriram EPC, Sula Wines, Shaadi.com and Genesis Colors.read less
