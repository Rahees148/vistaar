Customer Name: Dotturaj

Loan Amount: Rs 13 lakhs

Business: Twisting Machine

Mr. Dotturaj had a vision of Manufacturing and Supplying raw Materials for the Power loom Industry which was located in Ellampillai region. In early 2000's his family lives in Ellampillai in a rented place and he used to help his Father in Work. In the Year 2005 Dodduraj with his Brother became a Dealer by Procuring Jerry in Bulk from Surat and supplying it to the Power loom Owners. In Mid 2006 he purchased his First Twisting Machine to make Jerry and supply it to the Saree Manufacturers, that was the first time he started getting Profits. By 2009 the machine count has become 4 and the the Profits increased to 300% between 2005 and 2009. Gradually his Families standers of living has been changed ever since he started getting Profits. In 2012 he Built his own House also owns a Small Land and owns 8 Pressure Twisting Machines which can produce a high quality Jerry. He is been in Jerry Business since 10 Years ever since he started as a Dealer.

Testimonial

“Over 15 years ago I learnt the basic Operational work from my father and in 2006 i started my own Twisting Machine Business to cater the Jerry need for the Saree Manufacturers. I generally take small amount of Loan from the Local Lenders for my regular Stock purchase and it is becoming difficult for me to pay actual and interest separately. But when Vistaar Executives explained about their unique Credit system of giving loans by analyzing respective Businesses. That actually made me to take a risk of Availing a Bigger amount of Loan for Constructing Manufacturing Facility. For my future Aspirations i've availed a Business Loan from Vistaar and utilized for Constructing Manufacturing Facility and regular Stock purchase. My Business is in doing great, i am one among the Valued Customers of Vistaar by paying the EMI on time with no Cheque bounces and I am also looking forward to take an another loan from Vistaar. Now I could able to pay Actual and Interest at one time in simple Emi's.”

Timeline
  • 2013

    Business has grown from 50000 per month in 2005 to 6 lacs per month in 2013.

  • 2014

    Took Loan From
    Vistaar RS.13,00,000/-

  • Mid-2014

    Constructed Manufacturing Facility.

  • 2015

    Started Purchasing Stock without credit
    (As the revenue increased and he doesn't has to pay the rent for Manufacturing facility)

  • Mid-2015

    Business has grown 133% since 2013 from last 6 Lacs to 8 Lacs.

    Purchase Culting Machine

    Purchase 7 Twister Machines and make the total count to 15
